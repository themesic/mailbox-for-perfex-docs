## ğŸ“§ Using Preview Pane

### Viewing Emails

1. Click any email in the list
2. Email content appears in the preview pane
3. Scroll to read full email
4. Click another email to switch preview

### Navigation

- **Click email in list**: Shows in preview pane
- **Arrow keys**: Navigate up/down through emails
- **Scroll**: Scroll preview pane to read content

### Quick Actions

From the preview pane, you can:
- **Reply**: Click Reply button
- **Forward**: Click Forward button
- **Archive**: Click Archive button
- **Delete**: Click Delete button
- **Star**: Click star icon
- **Tag**: Select tags from dropdown
- **Mark as Important**: Click important icon

### Auto-mark as Read

When preview pane is enabled:
- Emails can be automatically marked as read when previewed
- Controlled by **Auto-mark as Read on Preview** setting
- Can be set globally or per-staff

**To Configure:**
1. Go to **Setup â†’ Settings â†’ Mailbox Settings**
2. Find **Auto-mark as Read on Preview**
3. Select **Yes** or **No**
4. Or configure per-staff in **Mailbox Config**
